What inspired us?


As fellow students who engages in conducting a study, we gained a first hand experience on how challenging it can be to conduct a study. This experience inspired us to create a web application that assist students in conducting their own studies.

Our Goal


Our main aim is to help students with their studies and gather useful feedback to make our web application even better. We want to make sure that students have all the support they need to succeed in their academic journey. By listening to their experiences and suggestions, we hope to keep improving our web application to make it more user-friendly and effective. Your input is crucial in shaping the future of our platform, and we're committed to making it a helpful and accessible tool for all students.

What is our website about?


Research Study Assistance Platform (RSAP) is a web app that contains research related features that seeks to provide assistance to students in conducting their own studies. This features includes a progress tracker, a compilation of research gateways, and many more. It has a user-friendly interface aimed specifically for students.
