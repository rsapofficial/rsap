APA Citation


BibCitation is a user-friendly APA citation tool for quick and accurate reference formatting. Ideal for students and professionals, it simplifies the citation process, ensuring precision and adherence to APA guidelines. Streamline your work and maintain citation consistency with BibCitation.


Research Ariticle Links


Google Scholar


Google Scholar is a freely accessible web search engine that indexes scholarly articles, theses, books, conference papers, and patents. It provides researchers with a centralized platform to explore and access a vast array of academic resources, facilitating efficient and comprehensive literature reviews.


ERIC


ERIC.gov.ph is the official website of the Education Research Information Center (ERIC) in the Philippines, serving as a centralized hub for researchers. It provides a comprehensive repository of educational research materials, facilitating easy access to valuable resources and fostering collaboration among scholars in the pursuit of academic excellence.


Science Direct


ScienceDirect is an online platform and repository offering access to a wide range of peer-reviewed scientific, technical, and medical research articles across various disciplines. Developed by Elsevier, it serves as a valuable resource for researchers, academics, and professionals to access and disseminate scientific knowledge.


Research Gate


ResearchGate is a professional network and platform designed for the global scientific community, enabling researchers to share and access academic publications, collaborate with peers, and engage in discussions across various disciplines.


Doaj.org


DOAJ.org, the Directory of Open Access Journals, is an online platform that provides a curated and freely accessible index of high-quality, peer-reviewed scholarly journals. It aims to promote open access to academic research, facilitating global dissemination and discovery of knowledge across various disciplines.


Taylor & Francis


Taylor & Francis is a leading academic publishing company that specializes in providing scholarly books, journals, and online resources across various disciplines. Known for its commitment to disseminating high-quality research and knowledge, Taylor & Francis serves the global academic community by publishing a diverse range of content and facilitating the exchange of ideas among researchers, scholars, and professionals.


EBSCO


EBSCO, which stands for Elton B. Stephens Company, is a global provider of research databases, e-journals, and other information resources. Specializing in academic, professional, and educational content, EBSCO facilitates access to a vast array of scholarly materials, offering valuable resources for students, researchers, and professionals across various disciplines.


JSTOR


JSTOR, short for "Journal Storage," is a digital library that provides access to a vast collection of academic journals, books, and primary source materials. It serves as a resource for scholars, researchers, and students, offering a comprehensive repository of scholarly content spanning various disciplines, including the arts, humanities, social sciences, and sciences. JSTOR facilitates convenient and organized access to a wealth of academic literature, fostering research and intellectual exploration.
